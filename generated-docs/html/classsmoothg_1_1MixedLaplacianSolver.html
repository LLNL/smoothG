Solve the graph Laplacian problem

The BlockVectors here are in "dof" numbering, rather than "truedof" numbering. That is, dofs on processor boundaries are repeated in the vectors that come into and go out of this method.

Implemented in smoothg::HybridSolver, and smoothg::MinresBlockSolver.
